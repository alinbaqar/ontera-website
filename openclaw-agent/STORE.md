# Store Profile — AmericanClothingDepot

## What We Sell

AmericanClothingDepot is an eBay store focused on men's fashion. Our main product categories are:

- **Streetwear** — graphic tees, hoodies, joggers, jackets, and urban fashion
- **Sports jerseys** — NFL, NBA, MLB, soccer, and other league jerseys
- **Hoodies & sweatshirts** — pullover and zip-up styles, branded and unbranded
- **Hats & caps** — snapbacks, fitted caps, beanies

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing. Most items fall in the $20–$80 range depending on category and brand.

## Shipping

- Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on item weight, dimensions, and buyer location.
- We ship within **1–2 business days** of receiving payment.
- Standard domestic delivery is typically **3–7 business days** after shipment.
- We provide tracking numbers for every order through eBay.
- International shipping availability varies by listing.

## Returns & Exchanges

We follow eBay's standard return policy:

- **30-day returns accepted** on most items.
- Items must be returned in original, unworn condition with tags attached.
- Buyer pays return shipping unless the item was not as described or defective.
- Refunds are processed within **2–3 business days** of receiving the return.
- We do not offer direct exchanges — buyer should return the item and purchase the correct one separately.

## Where We Ship From

United States.
