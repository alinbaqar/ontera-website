Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to make OnteraAI work for you. We never sell your data. We use industry-standard security to protect your information.

1. Introduction

OnteraAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered e-commerce automation platform.

By using OnteraAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an OnteraAI account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Billing information (processed securely by our payment provider)

Connected Platform Data

When you connect your e-commerce platforms (eBay, Amazon, Shopify, etc.), we access:

  • Customer messages and inquiries
  • Order information and fulfillment data
  • Product listings and inventory
  • Sales and transaction data
  • Store settings and policies

Important: We use secure OAuth connections provided by each platform. We never see or store your platform passwords.

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Device information
  • Feature usage statistics
  • AI interaction history (messages sent, actions taken)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our service: Respond to customer messages, create listings, manage orders, and generate reports on your behalf
  • Improve OnteraAI: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
  • Communicate with you: Send service updates, daily briefings, and support responses
  • Process payments: Handle subscription billing through our secure payment provider
  • Ensure security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), payment processing, and analytics tools that help us operate OnteraAI
  • Connected platforms: The e-commerce platforms you authorize us to access (we send data back to them on your behalf, like customer message replies)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 for platform connections
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Isolated customer environments

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your account information is deleted within 30 days
  • OAuth tokens are revoked immediately
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytics

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disconnect: Revoke platform connections at any time
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Third-Party Platforms

OnteraAI integrates with third-party e-commerce platforms (eBay, Amazon, Shopify, etc.). Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their policies:

  • eBay Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Privacy Notice
  • Shopify Privacy Policy

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure security

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

OnteraAI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the OnteraAI dashboard.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us:
