About OnteraAI

We're building the AI operating system that lets e-commerce sellers focus on growing their business, not managing it.

Our Story

OnteraAI was born from frustration. As an e-commerce seller running multiple stores across eBay, Amazon, and Shopify, I found myself spending more time answering the same customer questions and managing listings than actually growing my business.

I tried hiring virtual assistants, but the costs added up quickly—$500 to $2,000 per month—and the quality was inconsistent. Training new VAs every few months became exhausting.

So I built OnteraAI: an AI agent that handles the repetitive work with consistent quality, 24/7 availability, and at a fraction of the cost. It's the tool I wished existed when I was drowning in customer messages at 2 AM.

Our Mission

E-commerce sellers shouldn't have to choose between scaling their business and maintaining their sanity. We believe AI can handle the operational overhead—customer service, listing creation, order management—so sellers can focus on what they do best: finding great products and building their brand.

OnteraAI is the foundation your e-commerce business runs on. One AI agent. All your stores. Always on.

The Name

Ontera combines "on" (active, running) with "tera" (from Latin terra, meaning foundation or earth). It also evokes "terabyte"—the scale of modern digital commerce.
